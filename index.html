Brain Snacks & Breaks

Here’s a bunch of low-effort, high-vibe stuff to feed your brain something better than algorithm sludge. No, it’s not a productivity hack or self-help nonsense. Just some fun, random things to keep your mind.

🎧 What I Do To Avoid Rotting

Not about installing the Forest app or setting Instagram reminders every 15 minutes — that’s just extra stress.

  • Clickity-clack with my mechanical keyboard — because nothing says “I’m being productive” like the satisfying thock of keys. (Yes, I’m basically on monkeytype.com.)
  • Mood lighting wizardry — got a color-changing light and I use blue to focus, red when I’m officially brainrotting in bed, and pink just to feel cute AF.
  • I start with a one-minute plank, but somehow it turns into a full 30-minute Pilates session — go figure.
